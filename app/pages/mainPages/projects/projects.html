Nexus 7 Tablet at Conceus Inc. Brampton

The Nexus 7 Tablet was to be used in the Medical Operation Room, to have it display Neuro-Muscular Stimuli data from a medical device connected to the patient's body by muscular sensors.

This Nexus 7 Tablet came without any user interface, though an Android browser was present. My job was to build the HTML5 User interface, controlled by JavaScript and JQuery and styled with plain Cascading Style Sheets.
